About Vegas Vipers

The Vegas Vipers are a mountain bike composite MTB race team based in Las Vegas, Nevada. We're passionate about the sport and dedicated to developing riders of all skill levels. Our team competes in various mountain biking events throughout Nevada and surrounding states.

Performance

We focus on developing competitive riders through structured training and coaching.

Community

We build a supportive community of riders who share knowledge and encourage each other.

Safety

We prioritize rider safety through proper training, equipment, and trail etiquette.
